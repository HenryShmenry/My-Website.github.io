About me:
If you would like to know about my professional history, please check my CV page. Otherwise, my name is Henry Wise. I am a second-year mathematician currently in my second year of a Master of Mathematics Degree (MMath) at the University of York. As you have found my website you will have noticed that one of my current interests is Web Development! I also currently enjoy: 3D Design (Blender), Python, VScode (for webdev and this site), Overleaf (for LaTeX formatting and Tikz graphing), editing videos in Davinci Resolve, Writing the occasional long-form emails and Factory Games (Such as Satisfactory).
I'm currently an active voice actor on the website Casting Call Club (making use of Audacity), and the leader of a second year study group for the modules: Quantum & Continuum Dynamics, Groups Rings & Fields, and Linear Algebra. Recently, I stepped down after a year as the Secretary of the Mathematics society, wherein I was required to make meeting minutes, weekly emails, organize meetings and keep the emails up to date. I am still involved with the society in assisting the new secretary and helping with committee "handover". In the past, I have been heavily involved in open evenings/days in my sixth form (representing the Mathematics department) and my secondary school (representing the physics and triple sciences). I have a GitHub detailed in Contacts, where I occasionally update my TeX. Outside of coding, I am currently Tutoring an A-level student in mathematics aiming for an A with the AQA exam board.
I am currently reading Quantum Mechanics: The Theoretical Minimum by Leonard Susskind & Art Friedman.
